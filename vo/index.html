ABOUT


Grammy® Award-winning artist T.J. Troy comes equipped with 20+ years as a professional musician and performer, and a natural bass voice with considerable range and deep resonance, making him the perfect narrator/announcer. T.J.'s natural musicianship give him great control across his entire range and give birth to a wealth of characters, both dark/dramatic and comedic, as well as light and fun accent-driven voices.

His experiences with companies such as Cirque du Soleil and The Industry, along with ensembles such as Partch, MESTO, and Mamak Khadem Ensemble, have refined T.J.'s skill set to the highest standard of professional quality: easy and pleasant to communicate with, T.J.'s speed and grace in the studio have brought him accolades from producers and engineers across the industry.
